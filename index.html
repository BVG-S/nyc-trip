The few things that shape the trip

Decisions & heads-ups
  1. Order  Kept in your sequence. One forced exception: Adel's Halal opens 6 PM (and sits in Midtown), so it lands Saturday evening next to Din Tai Fung instead of midday.
  2. Your call  Your 10 PM Sat Din Tai Fung overlaps the July 4 fireworks (~9:25 PM, near the Seaport). Plan keeps DTF as you set it — shift it earlier only if you'd rather catch the show.
  3. Hours  NY Dosas is 11 AM–3 PM, closed Sundays (your Friday slot is fine). Russ & Daughters closes 4 PM (Sunday morning is fine).
  4. Diet  Mei Lai Wah's famous bun is roast pork — none of you. Optional sweet-bun stop only.
  5. Sun  Your three Sunday stops zig-zag across town before the 3 PM bus — doable but rushed; Thai Diner is the easiest to drop. Late trains run past midnight — confirm in TrainTime and line up a Poughkeepsie pickup.
